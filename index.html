Everything you need to know

ValidateIt has two modes, idea history, PDF reports and more. Here's how to get the most out of it.

01
✍️
Describe your idea
Type your business idea in plain English. The more specific you are, the better the analysis. Mention who you're targeting, what problem you're solving, and how you plan to make money.
💡 Bad: "a food app". Good: "a subscription meal kit for working mothers in Indian tier 2 cities costing ₹500/month"
02
⚔️
Choose your mode
ValidateIt has two modes. Single Idea mode validates one idea and gives it a score out of 100. Compare mode lets you paste two ideas head to head and the AI picks the winner with reasons.
💡 Use Compare mode when you can't decide between two ideas — it's the fastest way to pick the better one.
03
📊
Read your analysis
You get a viability score, market fit analysis, top 3 risks, revenue model suggestions, and a competitor breakdown. Every section is written by AI trained to be brutally honest — no sugarcoating.
💡 A score below 40 doesn't mean quit — it means pivot. Read the risks section carefully.
04
📄
Download your report
Hit "Download PDF" to get a professionally formatted report you can share with co-founders, mentors or investors. Your last 5 validations are also saved automatically so you can come back to them.
💡 Use the PDF report as a discussion document with your team before building anything.
